Data wrangling and missing value treatment

  • Out of total records(7043), 11 records have missing values for TotalCharges varibles. Hence, we will remove those 11 records from the dataset.
  • We will change No internet service to No for six columns, they are: OnlineSecurity, #OnlineBackup, DeviceProtection, TechSupport, streamingTV, streamingMovies.
  • We will change No phone service to No for column MultipleLines.
  • We will change the values in column SeniorCitizen from 0 or 1 to No or Yes.
  • We do not need column customerID for the analysis, hence will remove this column.

Correlation between numeric variables

  • Tenure and TotalCharges are highly correlated with a correlation value of more than 0.8. Hence, we will select Tenure as one of the model parameters and exclude TotalCharges.
  • Tenure and MonthlyCharges are less correlated. Therefore, we will both these varibles as model parameters.
